Budget Uncertainty

Contributors: Nicholas Morris, Ruben Proano

A study on budget uncertainty is conducted using a non-linear mixed integer program in a monte carlo simulation under a repeated general factorial design to understand the adverse effects of (193) countries reducing their budget in terms of accessibility, profitability, and affordability of vaccines.

  • Date: August 2016 - December 2018
  • Client: Gates Foundation
  • Category: Vaccine Research

Risk Index

Contributors: Nicholas Morris, Ruben Proano

A study on vaccine expenditure risk is conducted by mining data from the world bank, international monetary fund, and global competitive index to construct principal components that model which (191) countries are more likely than others to cut funding.

  • Date: August 2016 - December 2018
  • Client: Gates Foundation
  • Category: Vaccine Research

Stock Market Portfolio

Contributors: Nicholas Morris

A study on stock portfolio optimization is conducted by using daily closing prices from Yahoo and a linear program to determine how your entire investment should be portioned amoung (4629) stocks to create a portfolio that is expected to achieve a desired profit margin at minimal volitility.

  • Date: Febuary 2018 - July 2018
  • Client: Nicholas Morris
  • Category: Optimization

Economic Efficiency

Contributors: Nicholas Morris

A study on macro-economic efficiency is conducted by pulling data from the world bank and using a linear program to determine which (154) countries had a relatively more efficient economy that others, across the years of 2000 to 2014.

  • Date: March 2018 - April 2018
  • Client: Nicholas Morris
  • Category: Optimization

Philadelphia Crime

  • Client: Nicholas Morris
  • Category: Machine Learning
